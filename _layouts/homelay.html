Welcome to TITANS Lab!

Transformative Innovation for Trustworthy AI and Network Security (TITANS) lab is part of the Department of Computer Science at the Southern Illinois University Carbondale , led by PI: Alvi Ataur Khalil. The TITANS Lab is dedicated to advancing research in cybersecurity, blockchain systems, and artificial intelligence. Our mission is to design secure, scalable, and intelligent technologies that address real-world challenges.
